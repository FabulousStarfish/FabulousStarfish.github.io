Frequently Asked Questions


1. How much is prize amount ?
The prize money Rs. 200 for each game.
2. Where can I buy tickets ?
You can buy tickets from our school
3. Can I play all the games with my ticket ?
Yes, you can play all the three games with a single ticket.
4. How long can I play with my ticket ?
Once you login, you can play for a total of 20 minutes with your ticket. If you logout in between, your remaining can be used when you log back in next time.
5. What if my internet connect is interrupted within the 20 minute slot?
You can log back in with the same ticket and use the remaining time to play.
6. What if my score for a game is lower than when I played it previously?
We will consider your highest score and not the latest score.
7. I am not able to login with my ticket, it says "Invalid Ticket", what should I do ?
Please check your ticket code and try again. All ticket codes have 8 characters - 6 alphabets followed by 2 digits.
Example - BCJEFG27
8. How do I collect the prize?
We will contact you and share the details for you to collect the prize
9. How many prizes are there ?
There are three prizes, one for the top scorer in each game.
